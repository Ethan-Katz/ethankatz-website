Button Bash!

Button Bash! is an exhilarating, fast-paced arcade game that challenges your reflexes and quick decision-making skills. In this game, players are tasked with tapping buttons as they light up on the screen, earning points for speed and accuracy. With its vibrant graphics and addictive gameplay, Button Bash offers endless hours of fun. Whether you're looking to kill some time or aiming to top the leaderboards, Button Bash is the perfect game for you. Test your reflexes, improve your speed, and bash those buttons!

Holy Stride

Discover a unique way to intertwine fitness and faith as you traverse the beautiful landscapes of Israel. The app translates your steps into mitzvot according to halacha, honoring the tradition where every 4 amot walked equals one cherished mitzvah. Track your steps, embrace the spiritual essence of each stride, and embark on a journey that harmonizes physical activity with religious significance.
